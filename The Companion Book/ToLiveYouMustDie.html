To Live, You Must Die

To live you must die
Live fully and die

The fear of death prevents the experience of life

In order to be rid of the fear of death
You must die

To live you must die
To die you must first live
And to live you must wake

To get what you most deeply desire you have to experience what you most deeply fear

To live you must die

One can die any moment,
and that is why you must live each moment
