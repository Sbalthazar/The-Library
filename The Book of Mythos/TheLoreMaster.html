The Lore Master

The Lore Master is a seeker of knowledge and a guardian of wisdom.
 
The Lore Master knows the hidden wisdom of humanity.
He knows how to weave a new story for man.
He understands the past, knows the present and can therefore create the future.
 
The wisdom and knowledge is available if we can bring it together and synthesize it, and make it available for the modern man and the world at large.
 
He has learned much from books, but he doesn't remain in the dark, dusty library.
He goes out into the world and transforms dead words into living truths
