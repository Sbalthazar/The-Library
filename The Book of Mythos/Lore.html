Lore

Knowledge is power
 
Lore is the secret keys and arcana hidden in dusty tomes in forgotten libraries.
Those who dig find wonderful mysteries which have been discovered and shared by those who have come before.
 
Lore is the wisdom of the ages.
 
Contained within the Lore of Man is the whole Story of Man. 
 
It is the collective wisdom of Mankind, expressed in story.
 
The Book of Lore contains many secret meanings which unfold for one who reads it well and participates in it's mysteries.
 
Deeds become a story
   the story becomes history,
   history becomes legend,
   and legend becomes myth. 
 
Within letters, words, and books is contained this ancient and eternal lore.
