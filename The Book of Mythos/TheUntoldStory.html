The Untold Story/h1>
There is a Story that remains yet untold.
A story that longs to be told,
    but requires a master storyteller to tell it
A master creator and weaver to weave it.
 
The story exists as a potential and a longing in the hearts of Man.
It is a story that will be told someday.
 
It is The Story
It is the ultimate story
The Story of Stories
The unwritten tale and the unsung song
 
The story of humanity, of Man
The story of our journey from poverty to divine richness.
The tale of our quest for absolute freedom
The story of our ultimate triumph over darkness, evil, ignorance, limitation, and fear
The story of our apotheosis as we become gods. 
The Story of how Man rises to full consciousness and attains the ability to create a new reality.
