The Story of the New Earth

Writing The Story of The New Earth
 
Telling the Untold Story of Man
Speaking the unspoken story of Man
Weaving a new mythos for Man
 
Humanity is in desperate need of a New Story, a new Mythos.
A narrative and a vision of a golden future.
A story which makes sense of the past, informs the present and inspires us to the future.
A vision that the collective can rally around and build together.
A global consciousness and shared narrative.
 
A story which is of a totally new paradigm - a paradigm not bound by the limitation of paradigms.
A paradigm-less paradigm.
 
And the individual requires a new formulation of Man
Invoking and evoking the New Man. 
Inspiring within, and calling forth the New Man
