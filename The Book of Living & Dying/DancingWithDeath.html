Dancing with Death

I have danced with death,
    And courted her daily.
Though her kiss is cold,
    It is sweet.
And it is because of our silent dance,
     While holding my love tenderly in my arms,
That I am truly alive.
 
Her closeness,
   is my reminder.
Her breath,
   is my life.
